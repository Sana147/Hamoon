Pakistan Citizen Portal App

(State-sponsored App)

The Pakistan Citizen Portal app is a grievance redressal system that allows citizens to register their complaints against government officials or private citizens. As of 2025, the app had 5M+ downloads and a rating of 3.5 stars out of 5 on Google Play Store. The developer email ID is: pmdu@pmo.gov.pk and the app package is com.govpk.citizensportal.
