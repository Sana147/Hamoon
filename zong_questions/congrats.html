Congratulations! You have completed the training for the My Zong app and are now ready to use it in a more informed and secure manner.

Going forward, please keep these two security tips in mind:

  1. As an initial safety measure, because the My Zong app does not require a user-specified password, we strongly recommend that users use the "panic button" if their phone is stolen or confiscated. Activating the "panic button" will lock your phone and prevent an attacker from installing the My Zong app on the device to obtain call logs, SMS logs, and other sensitive user information. Users can use the built-in OS functionality for the "panic button" feature (several third-party "panic button" apps offer similar features as well).
  2. We also advise users to be cautious if they receive an OTP on their phone without initiating the request. This could be a sign of unauthorized access to their account, and users should take immediate action (e.g., locking the SIM or the phone) to secure it.
