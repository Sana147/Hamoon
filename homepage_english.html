Welcome to Hamoon!

Learn to protect yourself from app-based surveillance.

Hamoon teaches users about the security and privacy issues of seven popular Pakistani apps, and provides actionable advice on how to better protect against these threats.

Choose your own path to empowerment and enlightenment below! Click on the Cartoon Story to follow the tale of three friends as they learn more about these serious app-related risks. Think you know enough already? Select Test Your Knowledge to dive straight into a series of quizzes on the seven popular apps studied during this project.

Before you begin, remember to check out the Background and About tabs at the top of this page to learn more about the origin of the Hamoon project and the Pakistani-specific context in which these apps—and their users—operate.
