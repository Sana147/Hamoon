Congratulations! You have completed the training for the My Telenor app.

We are thrilled you completed the training and hope you found it enjoyable! We certainly had a great time putting together the training module for you.

As a safety measure, we strongly recommend that users use the "panic button" if their phone is stolen or confiscated. That is because the My Telenor app does not require a user-specified password. Activating the "panic button" will lock your phone and prevent an attacker from installing the UPTCL app on the at-risk user device to obtain call logs, SMS logs, and other sensitive user information. Users can use the built-in OS functionality for the "panic button" feature, or several third-party "panic button" apps offer similar features.

We also advise users to be cautious if they receive an OTP (One-Time Password) on their phone without initiating the request. That could be a sign of unauthorized access to their account, and users should take immediate action (e.g., locking the SIM or the phone) to secure it.
